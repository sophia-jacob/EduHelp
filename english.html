Flashcards

What is a verb?
A word that expresses an action or state of being.
What is a homophone?
Words that sound the same but have different meanings and spellings.
What is the subject of a sentence?
The person, place, or thing that the sentence is about.
What is a metaphor?
A comparison that says one thing is another without using "like" or "as".
What is an antonym?
A word that has the opposite meaning of another word.
What is a proper noun?
The name of a specific person, place, or thing.
What is a conjunction?
A word that joins words, phrases, or clauses.
What is an interjection?
A word or phrase that expresses emotion.
What is a fragment?
An incomplete sentence that is missing a subject, verb, or complete thought.
What is personification??
Giving human traits to nonhuman objects.
